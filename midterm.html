Entry and Sale Guidelines

Book Sale Room Guide © Friends of the Palo Alto Library

How to Enter Main Room:
For entry to the Main Room, numbered tickets will be given out from 8-11 am. Maximum of two tickets per customer. Customers will queue at the North Door and be admitted according to ticket number. Once inside, customers may make their selections, after which they should purchase these and exit via the East Door. These customers may then join the end of any remaining line at the North Door and reenter in that order.

Main Room Purchasing Limitations:
In the Main Room, there will be limitation of twelve books per customer up until 11 am on Saturdays. If there is still a line queueing at the North Door at 11 am, the twelve book limitation will continue. If there is no line after 11 am, customers may exceed the twelve books per customer limit.

At this time, there are no entry or purchasing limitations for the Bargain Room or Children's Room.
